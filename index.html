Make Your Own College Rankings

Below is a simple Angular app that offers a tounge-in-cheek look at college-ranking lists which, once small in number, have become myriad. The app democratizes the process. Check the appropriate boxes to create a college-ranking list that takes into account the characteristics your find important. Your results will appear in the right column.

Note: Each characteristic is based on a real 2014-2015 college ranking, the maker of which appears in italic.
